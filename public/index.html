Our Mission

"I feel like I am at home, but I know I am making a difference"
- Rishi Basdeo, President

Established in 2009, the East Ridge High School Knight and Nerdy Robotics team has held steadfast amongst its peers in the FIRST Robotics Competition. First Sponsored by Rick Croteau, it has shifted to Justin King as the head teacher of the team. The team has been working hard in the past 7 years to have an impact not only on FIRST, but on it’s community as well.

Students

Mechanical

The mechanical branch focuses on the physical aspects of the robot, it’s structure, it’s moving parts, and it’s maintenance. Overall, the mechanical team educates new members on the usage of pneumatics, motors, and gearboxes, along with other basic mechanics and tools.

Electrical/Programming

The electronics Branch is separated into two departments, programming and it’s namesake, electrical. These departments are closely intertwined, as one cannot function without the other, and they must work together to form the input and output of the robot.

In Competition

"It's fun to learn, but it's more fun to win"
- Alex Vila, Vice President

FIRST focuses on sparking the minds of students in science and technology while preparing them with life skills. FIRST has two core values, GraciouS Professionalism (GP) and Coopertition. GP encourages high quality work, emphasizes the value of others, and respects individuals throughout the competition. Coopertition is displaying unqualified kindness and respect in the face of fierce competition. These two values are combined together to create the balance of competition, teamwork and fun.
